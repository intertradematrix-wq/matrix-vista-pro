# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Corporate and B2B clients looking for reliable, comprehensive AV/IT solutions.

## Product Purpose

To showcase Matrix Intertrade's expertise, solutions, products, and past projects to potential clients. It acts as a professional digital storefront and portfolio. Success is measured by establishing trust and generating B2B leads.

## Brand Personality

Professional, Modern, Trustworthy.

## Anti-references

Too cluttered or complex layouts. Overwhelming walls of text or dense data that cause cognitive overload. Should not look like a cheap retail e-commerce store.

## Design Principles

- **Clarity over Density:** Space is a feature. Give content room to breathe to project confidence and professionalism.
- **Expert Confidence:** The design should feel established and premium, relying on strong typography and structural alignment rather than flashy decorations.
- **Show, Don't Tell:** Use high-quality imagery of past projects and products to speak for the brand's capability.
- **B2B Trust:** Clean, consistent, and predictable patterns that assure corporate clients of reliability.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Focus on high readability, clear and legible fonts, comfortable color contrast, and fully responsive layouts that work seamlessly across all devices.
